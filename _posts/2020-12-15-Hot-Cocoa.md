# Ingredients

    1 cup Milk

    2 tsp Cocoa Powder

    3 tsp Honey or sugar

    Pinch of salt

# Procedure 

> -  This recipe is a ratio. Make more than just one cup.
> -  Heat up the milk in a pot in low medium heat. Don't let it boil. 
> -  Whisk in the cocoa powder
> -  pinch of salt
> -  sweeten it to your taste
